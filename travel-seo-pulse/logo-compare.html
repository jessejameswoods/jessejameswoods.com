Honest take

Hand-coded SVG monograms have real limits — kerning, stroke weight balance, and optical adjustments that a type designer handles instinctively are hard to nail programmatically. If any of these directions feel right but need polish, that's a 30-minute job for a designer (or Figma). Option 3 (the badge) will read best at 40px because of the solid background + high contrast. Option 1 has the most personality. Option 2 is the middle ground.
